# Initial Concept
Intensive 3-month study project to transition from Senior Developer to Senior QA Automation Engineer, targeting international markets.

# Product Definition

## Vision
To bridge the gap between Software Development and Quality Assurance, leveraging 8 years of development experience to master modern automation frameworks, CI/CD pipelines, and QA methodologies. The ultimate goal is to secure a Senior QA Automation role in the global market.

## Target Audience
- **Primary User:** Senior Software Developer (8+ years exp) with a CS degree.
- **Goal:** Upskill in QA Automation tools and methodologies within 3 months.

## Core Competencies (Based on Market Analysis)
The curriculum is strictly aligned with high-standard industry requirements (e.g., Scanntech Senior QA Role):

1.  **Web UI Automation:**
    -   Mastery of modern tools: **Playwright** and **Cypress**.
    -   Competency in standard tools: **Selenium WebDriver**.
    -   Pattern implementation: Page Object Model (POM), Screenplay Pattern.

2.  **API & Service Automation:**
    -   Manual/Exploratory: **Postman**.
    -   Automated Code-based: **RestAssured** (Java) or Supertest/Axios (JS).
    -   Performance Testing: **K6** or JMeter.

3.  **CI/CD & DevOps:**
    -   Pipeline integration: **GitLab CI** and **GitHub Actions**.
    -   Containerization: **Docker** for test environments.
    -   Metrics: Implementing DORA metrics and Quality Gates.

4.  **QA Theory & Management:**
    -   Methodologies: Scrum/Kanban/SAFe.
    -   Artifacts: Test Plans, Bug Reports, Traceability Matrices.
    -   Standards: ISTQB Foundation/Advanced concepts.

## Success Criteria
-   **Portfolio:** A public GitHub repository demonstrating a full E2E test framework with CI/CD integration.
-   **Knowledge:** Ability to answer "Senior" level interview questions regarding architecture, strategy, and problem-solving.
-   **Speed:** Capability to set up a robust test scaffold from scratch in under 4 hours.
