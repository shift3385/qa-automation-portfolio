About the Role

As a Semi senior QA Engineer, you will help the team meet our standards for quality, This involves creating and executing test plans and automated tests, by yourself, and in collaboration with your team. You will also assist in reproducing bugs that are reported, to help with their resolution.

You will participate in team planning and design stages, to help the team build the best solutions that are also testable. This includes leveraging development environments and building automated tests, fixtures, and test data, in collaboration with their team and others.

The Tech Stack

Our applications use React, Apollo Client on the front end and Nodejs, graphql, Mysql, redis and elastic search which are split across multiple services. All of this lives in mono repo with "turn key" docker powered development environment allowing you as a developer to focus on what matters. Everything packed in a next generation PWA (Progressive Web Application) running in kiosk mode on Google Chrome OS devices, making us one of the first Google partners to build PWA's for the new ChromeOS web API's.

About EverPass: 

EverPass Media is a comprehensive media platform dedicated to commercial businesses, that aggregates, distributes and enables streaming of live sports and entertainment content, and offers a wide array of consumer engagement and performance marketing tools for bars, restaurants, hotels and other commercial venues.
